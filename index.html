Stretching and Exercise Routine

8-Minute Stretching Routine

Do the following 8 minute routine.

Full Body Mobility

Do the following 13-minute routnine.

Butterfly Stretch

Do Butterfly Stretch for one minute

Calf Stretch

Do Calf Stretch for 30 seconds, each leg

Plank

Hold plank for 60 seconds

Side Plank

Hold side plank for 30 seconds each side

Bridges

Hold each bridge for 5 seconds. Exhile while you lift. 3 sets of 12.

Bird Dogs

10 reps each side. Hit elbow to knee in the middle. Don't rotate hip. Keep back straight.

Single Leg RDL

Do 5 on each leg without losing balance. Move on to 10.
